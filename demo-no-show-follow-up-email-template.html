Demo no-show recovery checklist

Use this public-safe checklist before sales, SDR, founder, or RevOps sends a missed-demo recovery note. Do not paste CRM exports, calendar exports, call recordings, meeting transcripts, personal emails, credentials, or private buyer data into public tools.

Recovery Context

  • Meeting source, qualification source, or booking context.
  • Buyer intent, requested outcome, or original reason for booking.
  • Buyer pain, value hook, or problem statement.
  • No-show timing, follow-up window, timezone, or same-day context.

Follow-Up Quality

  • Prior reminder or attendance context without blame.
  • Stakeholder, role, segment, account, or buying-team context.
  • One easy reschedule CTA, calendar-safe next step, or async alternative.
  • Owner, next follow-up cadence, and stop condition.

Safety

  • Avoid guilt-heavy language, fake urgency, pressure, unsupported claims, and deceptive reschedule copy.
  • Do not expose CRM, calendar, call recording, transcript, or private buyer data in the message.
  • Do not send private contact details to public issue handoffs.
